What is Start The Up?

A community for students who want to stop consuming content about startups — and actually start building. We run structured cohorts, host events, and celebrate real product launches.

Our mission

Built for student builders who want to ship

Most student communities are about learning. Start The Up is about doing. We believe the best way to learn is to build a real product that real people can use.

Our cohort model keeps you accountable — small groups, twice-weekly check-ins, and a 4-week deadline that turns ideas into launched products.

What we believe in

Community values

🔨

Builders over talkers

We value people who show up, build, and share — over people who endlessly plan and never ship. Progress, not perfection.

🔓

Build in public

Share your progress, your failures, your wins. Transparency makes everyone better and builds real accountability.

🤲

Help first, ask second

Before asking for a favor, ask how you can help someone else. Strong communities are built on generosity.

📦

Ship something real

Landing pages don't count. We celebrate builders who put something into the world that real people can use.
